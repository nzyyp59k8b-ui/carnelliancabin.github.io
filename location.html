Trails & Outdoors
Adventure starts at your back door
Burton Creek State Park is right behind the cabin — miles of peaceful forested trails through towering Jeffrey pines, rushing creeks, and wildflower meadows. The Tahoe Rim Trail is minutes away with views that stop you in your tracks.
Burton Creek State Park
Your backyard trail system — a serene forested network with well-marked paths, rushing creeks, red wildflowers, and trails suitable for all ages. Popular with hikers and mountain bikers alike.
Hike · Bike · All levels · Right out back
Tahoe Rim Trail
The iconic 165-mile trail circling Lake Tahoe — with nearby access points for day hikes offering sweeping panoramic views of the cobalt blue lake and snow-capped Sierra Nevada peaks.
Hike · Bike · Intermediate – Advanced
Carnelian Bay Trail Network
17 local mountain bike trails weaving through the forest, including routes connecting all the way to Northstar Bike Park for a full day on two wheels.
Mountain Bike · All levels
Agate Overlook — Rockpile Ramble
A rewarding climb starting on Carnelian Woods Avenue with gorgeous Lake Tahoe views at the summit. Keep an eye out for hawks and eagles along the way.
Hike · Moderate
