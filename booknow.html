Getting Here
Finding The Carnelian Cabin

The cabin is located in Carnelian Bay on the North Shore of Lake Tahoe. The exact address will be provided upon booking confirmation — we'll make sure you have everything you need before arrival day!

🌉
From the Bay Area
Take I-80 East toward Sacramento, continue through Auburn and up the mountain to Truckee. Exit at Highway 267 South toward Kings Beach, then follow North Lake Blvd west along the lake to Carnelian Bay.

Approx. 3.5 – 4 hours
🏙️
From Sacramento
Take I-80 East toward Truckee. Exit at Highway 267 South toward Kings Beach, then follow North Lake Blvd west along the lake to Carnelian Bay.

Approx. 2 – 2.5 hours
